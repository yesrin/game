Studio Note

Lena Studio is a one-person indie outfit based in Seoul, focused on small games that respect your time. We make things that are easy to start, hard to put down, and never ask you to grind.

The studio was founded by a backend developer who got tired of building dashboards and started building toys. Every game ships with the same question in mind: does this make someone smile in 60 seconds or less?

Currently a team of one. Open to collaboration with artists, audio designers, and other indies who like making things small and well.
